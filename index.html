United Armenian Volunteers League

Hello, welcome to United Armenian Volunteers League


We are platform for volunteers, students and youth workers where they can self-express and self- educate. We organize professional seminars, conferences, round-table discussions and other events, which will help to receive non-formal education. Also, we organize courses where our memebers learn essential skills.
On the other hand we help businesses to organize various events. Thus, our projects offer students a chance to learn new skills and to have a professional network.

Armenian Blockchain Forum

Armenian Blockchain Forum was the first biggest blockchain event in Armenia. There were more than 600 attendees, 30 speakers from all over the world. It was a platform where specialists were able to share their experience and discuss the most important topics.

Thomas Anders and The Modern Talking Band Concert

On 27 th of April Thomas Anders have had a concert with the Modern Talking Band at Sports and Concert Complex after K. Demirchyan. There were more than 7000 attendees.

I’ll become a programmer

I’ll become a programmer was a training for the children from care houses from all over the Armenia. The whole training was divided into two parts: basic knowledge about programming and group work. There were specialists from the field, who both gave speeches and helped children to realize their ideas.


Mathematical Contest organized by ImDproc

ImDproc is an online educational platform. Each year they organize different contests on different subjects. The whole contest is divided into two phases. The participants are pupils from the school of all the Yerevan.
